Method in plain terms

Utility score = sum(scene-specific weight x interpretable feature value). Features are intelligibility, noise control, comfort, loudness stability, and speech prominence.

Feedback update changes only the selected scene's weights with bounded adjustment and regularization, then verifies other scenes remain stable under a score-shift threshold.

Illustrative prototype for conversation and fitting strategy exploration. Not a production hearing-aid algorithm.
